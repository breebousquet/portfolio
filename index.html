About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Bree Bousquet! I was born and raised in Winnipeg, MB, Canada. I moved to Thunder Bay in July 2008. I've wanted to pursue photography since I was young. I've always had a knack for taking pictures, filming, I just loved holding a camera.

I started IMD (Interactive Media Development) at Confederation College in September of 2017. In this program, I've discovered a liking for mobile app development, on top of photography. There's so much you can do once you know how to code an app, and it's so cool to see how apps really work. Some apps are so simplistic, but some (like Facebook or something with many pages to navigate to) are so intricate!
